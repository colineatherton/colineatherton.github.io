Meet The Team

Benjamin Kott

CEO & Founder

Prior to founding EnergyDeck, Benjamin headed up Green Business Operations and Clean Energy Advocacy at Google Europe. In this role, he was responsible for managing and reducing resource consumption of Google's operations, identifying and implementing large scale renewable energy investments and driving a major outreach campaign around climate change. He was also involved with the Google PowerMeter rollout in Europe. Before Google, Benjamin worked for Booz & Co on projects in the Media & Technology, Aerospace and Energy sectors in Italy and Europe. Prior to that he worked for EADS Airbus in Germany and France. Benjamin holds an M.Sc. degree in Aerospace Engineering from Technical University of Munich, and an MBA from Insead. He is on the sustainability advisory panel of the BBC.

Bruno Girin

CTO

Bruno has 15+ years' experience as Enterprise Solutions Architect and developer working on implementing large scale, distributed IT solutions in the UK, Europe and the Middle East. Throughout his career he worked for several blue-chip organisations including Lloyds Banking Group, Royal Bank of Scotland, BNP Paribas Real Estate, BT Global Services, CSC and Reuters. He also used to be Chief Technical Architect of startup Cambista Technologies over a period of four years. Bruno is an expert in all things Agile and Accessibility, and holds an M.Sc. in Computer Science from TELECOM Nancy in France.

Nic Mason

COO

Nic is an expert in commercial real estate and public sector carbon and energy management. Prior to EnergyDeck he worked at CBRE, where he was responsible for developing and implementing the data management and evidence gathering processes that underpin carbon reporting requirements for a range of corporate and local authority clients in the UK and across EMEA. He was also focused on delivering energy efficiency strategies for large property portfolios, specific energy efficiency project implementation for individual buildings; and sustainable procurement and energy procurement strategies. Previously, Nic worked for Westminster City Council in a range of roles spanning energy, water, carbon and sustainability management. Nic holds an MBA and a Master of Arts in Political Science, both from University of Canterbury in New Zealand.

Magalie Durio

Project Manager - Commericial Real Estate

Prior to joining EnergyDeck, Magalie worked in the field of business development and marketing at Open Ocean, a marine renewable energy consultancy. She played an important part in the creation of the MRE World Map, which is today the most detailed and up-to-date listing of all marine renewable energy projects around the world. Magalie also worked at Qudini, a start-up providing reservation and wait list management software solutions for restaurants and retailers, where she was in charge of marketing and business development activities. Magalie is in the process of obtaining a Master's Degree in Business Engineering from Telecom Ecole de Management in France.
